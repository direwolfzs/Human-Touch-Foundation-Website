About Us

 

 

Human Touch Foundation is a social and emotional outreach group that supports cancer patients throughout their jounrey. While millions of dollars are being poured into Cancer Research, the cancer patients themselves are forgotten. A cancer patient may spend weeks in a hospital, alone and left to think of their future. Without companionship, they are often left hopeless and lost. Resonating from the hearts of a few in Dutchtown High School, the Human Touch Foundation now hopes to establish and expand nationally to touch every heart struggling with Cancer.
